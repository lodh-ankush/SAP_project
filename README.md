# SAP Record-to-Report (R2R) Financial Analytics Project

## Overview
This project demonstrates the **Record-to-Report (R2R)** business process in **SAP Financial Accounting (FI)** along with a **mock analytics dashboard** built using sample financial data.

The objective of the project is to understand how organizations record financial transactions, generate financial statements, and use analytics to support business decision-making.

The project simulates a fictitious company and covers the **complete financial closing cycle**, including journal entries, accounts payable, accounts receivable, financial reporting, and KPI-based analysis.

## Business Scenario

A fictitious company **ABC Manufacturing Pvt Ltd** is used to simulate financial operations for an electronics manufacturing business.

Key financial activities covered:
- capital investment recording
- purchase transactions
- vendor payments
- customer invoices
- salary expenses
- asset accounting
- bank reconciliation
- financial reporting

## Analytics Component

A mock dataset representing 12 months of financial activity was created to perform analytics.

### KPIs calculated
- Total Revenue
- Total Expense
- Net Profit
- Profit Margin %
- Expense Ratio %
- Monthly Revenue Growth

### Dashboard Visualizations
- Revenue Trend (Line Chart)
- Profit Trend (Line Chart)
- Expense Distribution (Pie Chart)
- Revenue vs Expense (Bar Chart)

These analytics help identify:
- financial growth patterns
- cost-heavy operational areas
- profitability improvement trends
- expense optimization opportunities

## Key Insights

- Revenue increased from ₹4.2 lakh to ₹8.2 lakh across 12 months
- Profit improved significantly due to controlled expense growth
- Purchase cost is the largest expense component
- Profit margin improved steadily over time
- Financial analytics enables better budgeting and planning decisions
